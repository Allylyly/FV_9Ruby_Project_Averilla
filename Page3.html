March: Fire Prevention Month


March is Fire Prevention Month because of the fact that this month is one of the hottest months of the year, with summer coming not too long after this month. Besides the fact that the temperature, along with humidity reach maximum level during March, it is also the month that has very many fire accidents, if being compared to the other months. to further read more, please click on the topmost title following "How to Prevent Fires".
